Interpretation
--------------
- English Phase 2 remains mostly syntax-led even after the richer feature expansion.
- Chinese Phase 2 is the clearest case where the winning slice uses a more task-shaped subset rather than a broad universal pool.
- Greek Phase 2 is strongly acoustic/paralinguistic.
- Spanish Reading is almost entirely driven by the reading-reference block, which is the cleanest example of task-specific semantic features dominating.
