The Greek recordings assess the verbal fluency and mood of the
participant using a picture that the participant has to describe (with
the picture in the eyes) and then to involve a discussion about their
interests for the activity represented. In detail, the assessor shows to
the participant one picture representing daily activity and then asks
the participants to give a verbal description of the picture in a few
sentences. The assessor asks if the participant likes doing this
activity and why. The  picture represents a couple of older people
walking on the mountains.
