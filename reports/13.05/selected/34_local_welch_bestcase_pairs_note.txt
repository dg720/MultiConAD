Language-Specific Welch Best-Case Pairs
======================================
These examples use an HC-oriented local Welch saliency scale built from smoothed percentile ranks on the training split.
Selection rule per language:
- AD: correctly classified case with the lowest mean HC-oriented burden
- HC: correctly classified case with the highest mean HC-oriented burden

This is intended to show typical best-case near-empty AD and near-full HC profiles under the language-specific feature set.
Unlike the canonical overlay plots, this case-level view does not use the 5th-95th clipped burden transform; it uses percentile-style scoring to avoid hard floor effects.
