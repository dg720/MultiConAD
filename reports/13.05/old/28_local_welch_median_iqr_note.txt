Language-Specific Welch Median + IQR Overlay
===========================================
This plot mirrors the pooled median/IQR radar style, but each language uses its own Welch+Bonferroni+|d| top feature list.
The displayed burden scores remain AD-oriented on a 0-100 scale.
- solid line: per-language median burden for AD or HC
- shaded band: interquartile range (25th to 75th percentile)
- no individual sample traces are drawn
